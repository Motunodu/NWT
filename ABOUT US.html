    About us

    Get to know us

    Vision

    Our vision is to support quality education by providing resources and programs which will aid balanced learning in children living in underserved communities.


    Mission

    Read4Africa supports the teaching, learning, reading and donation of various textbooks on all subjects, dictionary, story books by ensuring that Public School Pupils and individuals have access to world-class resources.

    Information literacy

    Read4Africa team work closely with teachers to create opportunities for pupils to learn in a fun way

    Support and Resources

    An important role of this project team is to provide help and support to Public school pupils, most especially the Basic classes.

    The Read4Africa Project provides help and support in form of textbook, notebooks and other school supplies to pupils in underserved communities.


    Access to trusted educational material

    Our library Services provides books to school libraries and receives neatly used and new books from individuals who have or whose children have used books they are no longer using and these include educational books, historical books, fiction and story books, Dictionary and inspirational books for kids.
