    Welcome to Read4Africa

    Moulding the African child for a greater tomorrow.
    Read4Africa Project is a books and school supplies donating project. We target some selected public primary schools in Ibadan, Oyo state. We accept donation of new and neatly used educational books which are dropped off at our drop off centres and we buy books and other school supplies from the money donated to us, which we donate to the school children and the school library once every session. Presently we work with 10 carefully selected public primary school in Ibadan, Oyo State but we hope to expand our reach to other schools. please go to projects to see more of what we do.
