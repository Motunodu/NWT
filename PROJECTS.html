    Our Projects

    The different ways we reach out to our community

    In order to achieve our vision in Read4Africa Project, we have four projects we carry out on yearly basis. These projects include;

    Reading and book review club

    This project has to do with forming reading clubs in selected public primary schools to encourage the reading habits in children. This project involves giving books out to children to read at a specified time and they come together once a week to review each chapter and discuss lessons learnt. This is done in partnership with the school and the English or literacy teacher in that school.

    Literacy competition

    This project involves having a spelling bee and dictation competition, grammer competition and story writing completion among the pupils. This is an inter-school competition that comes up once a session.

    donation program

    This program has to do with donating different types of books to the school library and school supplies to school pupils such as notebooks, pen, pencil, eraser, sharpener,school sandals; and text book are donated to each class so as to be used by the teacher for a child whose parents cannot afford it.
